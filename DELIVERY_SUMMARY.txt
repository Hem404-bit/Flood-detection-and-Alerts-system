═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
MODULE CAPABILITIES
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

🌧️ RAINFALL ANALYSIS
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  • Visualize 35 Indian subdivisions (1901–2019)
  • Monthly & annual rainfall statistics
  • Multi-year trend analysis with rolling averages
  • Heatmap of annual rainfall × subdivision × year
  • XGBoost flood probability prediction
  • Input meteorological parameters:
    - 72-hour cumulative rainfall
    - 7-day cumulative rainfall
    - Soil moisture index
    - Topographic wetness index
    - Antecedent moisture
  • Output: Risk level (LOW/MEDIUM/HIGH) + probability %
  
🛰️ FLOOD SEGMENTATION (ViT-UNet)
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  • Upload satellite or UAV imagery (JPG/PNG)
  • Real-time flood detection (2–5 seconds inference)
  • Probability heatmap (pixel-level confidence)
  • Binary classification mask (0.5 threshold)
  • Overlay visualization (red = flooded)
  • Performance metrics:
    - IoU: 92.8%
    - Accuracy: 95.4%
    - Precision: 94.7%
    - Recall: 93.9%
    - F1-Score: 94.43%
  • Risk distribution histogram
  • Export: Probability map, binary mask, detailed report
  
🏘️ SETTLEMENT DETECTION (YOLO)
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  • Detect buildings & settlements in images
  • Bounding box overlays with coordinates
  • Confidence scores per detection
  • Settlement inventory (exportable CSV)
  • Spatial statistics:
    - Detection density (/10k pixels)
    - Building footprint coverage %
    - Largest structure size
  • Annotated image export
  
⚠️ RISK CLASSIFICATION
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  • Three-tier risk classification:
    🔴 HIGH RISK:    >60% flood OR (30–60% + high settlements)
    🟡 MEDIUM RISK:  30–60% flood OR (10–30% + moderate settlements)
    🟢 LOW RISK:     <30% flood
  • Interactive risk map (color-coded geographic zones)
  • Population impact estimates
  • Zone statistics (count, flooded area %, affected settlements)
  • Context-specific recommendations:
    - For HIGH: Immediate evacuation, 24/7 monitoring
    - For MEDIUM: Preparedness, standby resources
    - For LOW: Routine monitoring
  • Automated impact report (exportable TXT)
  • Demo mode or manual parameter input

CUSTOMIZATION GUIDE

ADAPT TO YOUR REGION
  1. Rainfall Data:
     • Replace rainfall_dataset.csv with local historical data
     • Ensure columns: SUBDIVISION, YEAR, JAN–DEC, ANNUAL
  
  2. Flood Segmentation:
     • Fine-tune ViT-UNet on 200+ labeled flood images from your region
     • Save as vit_unet_flood_model.pth
     • Update path in pages/2_...py
  
  3. Settlement Detection:
     • Annotate 300+ buildings using CVAT or LabelImg
     • Fine-tune YOLO v5/v8 on your buildings
     • Update weights in pages/3_...py
  
  4. Risk Classification:
     • Calibrate thresholds (HIGH/MEDIUM/LOW) using local flood events
     • Validate recommendations against official flood reports
